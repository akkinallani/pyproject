About Us

Our highly praised class curriculum and instruction include the following features:

  • Project and Game Based Learning Approach
  • Weekly Topic Review And Practices
  • Final Individal Student-Led Project To Showcase Learning

At The Py Project, we provide FREE tutoring to middle and elementary school students who want to learn the basics of computer programming. Our tutors are all dedicated high school students who have a passion for programming and are eager to share their knowledge. We believe that everyone should have access to the tools and skills needed to explore the world of computer programming. Our tutors are excited to help students of all ages learn the fundamentals of Python and help them gain confidence in their coding skills.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some FAQ that we usually get about our classes, curriculum and instruction. These are only FAQ, so if you have any additional questions, don't hesitate to contact us via WhatsApp or email.

When will classes start for the Summer Bootcamp?

The plan is to start them in early June, depending on student interest and enrollment.

What is covered in the classes for Beginner and Intermediate?

Beginner classes will cover all the basics in a relatively fast manner. These include concepts like printing, data types, variables, input and conditionals. Our intermediate class consists of a deep dive into loops and functions. Both classes end with students presenting a project that they made that showcases their new skills.

How long do classes last?

Classes are held once a week for 6 weeks. The 6th week will be a short class, where students present their final product. Each class is 1hr long, with the exception of week 6.

What happens if my student misses a class?

If your student misses a class, they are welcome to attend a different session that week. If they are unable to, classes can also be recorded.

How much do these classes cost?

These classes are completely FREE. However, when you are signing up, you can optionally donate a $25 fee. As we are a non-profit, we will use these funds to organize future events such as Hack-A-Thons at the end of the summer and career seminars. All profits will be donated to schools in India.
